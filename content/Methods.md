# Instructional Design

The migrated book compares two instructional conditions while keeping the conceptual scope aligned.

The classic condition presents gradient descent through textbook-style definitions and formulas. The multimodal condition presents the same ideas through geometric visualization, a physical analogy, a worked example, and interactive exploration. The shared exercise chapter can be used after either condition.

This scaffold page is excluded from the built book; the active instructional design is represented directly by the chapter order in `toc.yml`.
