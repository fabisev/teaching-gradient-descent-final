# Part I: Classic Textbook and Formula

This part introduces gradient descent in a classic textbook style. No previous knowledge of machine learning is assumed.

You will start with the basic words first, then move toward the update formula. The aim is to understand the idea before treating the symbols as something to memorize.

## What this part includes

- a beginner-friendly explanation of models, parameters, loss, gradient, and learning rate
- a theoretical explanation of gradient descent in one and many dimensions
- a text-based explanation of convergence, overshooting, and repeated updates
- a formal presentation of the update rule
- a short explanation of batch, stochastic, and mini-batch updates
- a small number of simple figures to support the explanation

## Subchapters

1. **Foundations** introduces the optimization problem and the core terms.
2. **Algebraic View** explains the update rule and its theoretical meaning in textbook form.
